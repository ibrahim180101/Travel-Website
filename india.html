10-Day Tour of India

India, renowned for its vibrant culture, diverse landscapes, and rich history, stands as a global tourist destination with its myriad attractions. From the bustling streets of Delhi to the serene backwaters of Kerala, India offers a captivating journey through its diverse regions. Explore iconic landmarks such as the Taj Mahal in Agra, the forts and palaces of Rajasthan, and the spiritual city of Varanasi. Delight your taste buds with the spicy flavors of Indian cuisine and immerse yourself in the warmth of Indian hospitality, making India a memorable destination for travelers seeking a blend of culture, history, and adventure.

Day By Day Itinerary

Itinerary description

Day 1: Arrival in Delhi

Arrive in Delhi, the capital city of India, and check into your hotel. Spend the day exploring the bustling streets of Old Delhi or visit historical sites like the Red Fort and Jama Masjid.

Day 2: Agra - Taj Mahal

Travel to Agra and visit the iconic Taj Mahal, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the Seven Wonders of the World. Explore the majestic Agra Fort and enjoy the evening witnessing the sunset over the Taj Mahal.

Day 3: Jaipur - Pink City

Travel to Jaipur, the vibrant capital of Rajasthan known as the Pink City. Explore the majestic Amber Fort, visit the City Palace, and shop for handicrafts and textiles in the bustling markets.

Day 4: Pushkar

Head to Pushkar, a sacred town famous for its Brahma Temple and the Pushkar Lake. Explore the vibrant streets, visit the ghats, and experience the evening aarti ceremony by the lake.

Day 5: Udaipur - City of Lakes

Travel to Udaipur, known as the City of Lakes. Explore the majestic City Palace, take a boat ride on Lake Pichola, and visit the Jagdish Temple and Saheliyon ki Bari.

Day 6: Jodhpur - Blue City

Travel to Jodhpur, the Blue City, and explore the majestic Mehrangarh Fort, Jaswant Thada, and the vibrant markets of the old town. Enjoy panoramic views of the city from the fort.

Day 7: Jaisalmer - Golden City

Head to Jaisalmer, the Golden City, known for its sandstone architecture and the majestic Jaisalmer Fort. Explore the narrow lanes of the old town, visit havelis, and enjoy a camel safari in the Thar Desert.

Day 8: Varanasi - Spiritual Capital

Take a flight to Varanasi, the spiritual capital of India. Explore the ghats of the sacred Ganges River, witness the mesmerizing Ganga Aarti ceremony, and visit the ancient temples and narrow alleys of the old city.

Day 9: Kerala Backwaters

Travel to Kerala and experience the serene backwaters on a traditional houseboat. Cruise through the tranquil network of canals, lagoons, and lakes surrounded by lush greenery and coconut palms.

Day 10: Departure from Delhi

Enjoy a final breakfast at your hotel before departing for your return journey from Delhi. Transfer to the airport for your flight back home with unforgettable memories of India. Namaste!
