8-Day Tour of the United Kingdom

The United Kingdom, renowned for its rich history, diverse culture, and stunning landscapes, stands as a top tourist destination. From the bustling streets of London to the picturesque countryside of the Lake District, the UK offers a captivating journey through its diverse regions. Explore iconic landmarks such as Big Ben, Buckingham Palace, and Stonehenge. Indulge in delicious British cuisine, from traditional fish and chips to afternoon tea, and immerse yourself in the country's cultural heritage, making the UK an unforgettable destination for travelers seeking history, natural beauty, and cultural experiences.

Day By Day Itinerary

Itinerary description

Day 1: Arrival in London

Arrive in London, the capital city of the United Kingdom, and check into your hotel. Spend the day exploring the historic streets of London, visit iconic landmarks such as Big Ben, the Houses of Parliament, and Buckingham Palace.

Day 2: London

Explore more of London's attractions today. Visit the British Museum to discover artifacts from around the world, take a ride on the London Eye for panoramic views of the city, and enjoy a West End musical in the evening.

Day 3: Oxford

Take a day trip to Oxford, renowned for its prestigious university and historic architecture. Explore the colleges of Oxford University, visit the Bodleian Library, and enjoy a leisurely walk along the River Thames.

Day 4: Bath

Travel to Bath, known for its Roman-built baths and Georgian architecture. Visit the Roman Baths, explore the charming streets lined with honey-colored buildings, and relax in the Thermae Bath Spa.

Day 5: Windsor

Head to Windsor and visit Windsor Castle, the oldest and largest inhabited castle in the world. Explore the State Apartments, St. George's Chapel, and enjoy a stroll around the beautiful Windsor Great Park.

Day 6: Lake District

Travel to the Lake District, a picturesque region known for its stunning lakes, mountains, and charming villages. Enjoy outdoor activities such as hiking, boating, or simply relax and take in the breathtaking scenery.

Day 7: Edinburgh

Take a train to Edinburgh, the capital city of Scotland. Explore Edinburgh Castle, stroll along the Royal Mile, and visit the Scottish Parliament. Don't forget to try some traditional Scottish cuisine, such as haggis or Scotch pie.

Day 8: Departure from London

Enjoy a final breakfast at your hotel before departing for your return journey from London. Transfer to the airport for your flight back home with unforgettable memories of the United Kingdom.
