8-Day Tour of France

France, renowned for its rich history, art, and culture, stands as a global tourist haven with its diverse attractions. The iconic Eiffel Tower in Paris, a symbol of love and romance, attracts millions of visitors annually. Beyond the capital, the picturesque landscapes of Provence, the charming vineyards of Bordeaux, and the historic châteaux of the Loire Valley offer a captivating journey through France's diverse regions. Culinary delights, from exquisite pastries to fine wines, contribute to the country's allure, making France a perennial favourite for travellers seeking a blend of sophistication and charm.

Day By Day Itinerary

Itinerary description

Day 1: Your arrival in France

Meet at the hotel restaurant for a cocktail reception at our boutique accommodation, Hotel Edgar Paris Offer includes: Dinner and unlimited beverages.

Day 2:leisure in Paris

Arrive in Paris and check into your hotel. Spend the day exploring the city at your own pace. You may want to visit iconic landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, or Notre-Dame Cathedral.

Day 3: beauty of Paris

Enjoy a guided tour of Paris, visiting famous attractions like the Arc de Triomphe, Champs-Élysées, and Montmartre. In the evening, take a relaxing Seine River cruise.

Day 4: Versailles

Take a day trip to the Palace of Versailles, a UNESCO World Heritage site known for its stunning gardens and opulent interiors. Return to Paris in the evening.

Day 5: Lyon

Travel to Lyon, the gastronomic capital of France. Explore the city's historic Old Town, sample local cuisine at traditional bouchons, and visit landmarks like Fourvière Basilica.

Day 6: Provence

Head to Provence and discover its picturesque countryside, charming villages, and lavender fields. Visit Avignon, known for its Papal Palace, and enjoy wine tasting in the Rhône Valley.

Day 7: Monaco

Take a day trip to Monaco, a glamorous principality known for its luxury casinos, yacht-lined harbor, and the famous Monte Carlo district. Return to Nice in the evening.

Day 9: Return Home
