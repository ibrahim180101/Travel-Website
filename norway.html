8-Day Tour of the United Kingdom

The United Kingdom, renowned for its rich history, diverse culture, and stunning landscapes, stands as a top tourist destination. From the bustling streets of London to the picturesque countryside of the Lake District, the UK offers a captivating journey through its diverse regions. Explore iconic landmarks such as Big Ben, Buckingham Palace, and Stonehenge. Indulge in delicious British cuisine, from traditional fish and chips to afternoon tea, and immerse yourself in the country's cultural heritage, making the UK an unforgettable destination for travelers seeking history, natural beauty, and cultural experiences.

5-Day Tour of Norway

Norway, renowned for its stunning fjords, majestic mountains, and vibrant cities, stands as a top tourist destination in Scandinavia. From the scenic beauty of the Norwegian fjords to the cultural attractions of Oslo, Norway offers a captivating journey through its diverse landscapes and rich heritage. Explore iconic landmarks such as the UNESCO-listed Bryggen Wharf in Bergen, the breathtaking Geirangerfjord, and the Viking Ship Museum in Oslo. Indulge in delicious Norwegian cuisine, from fresh seafood to traditional brunost, and immerse yourself in the country's outdoor adventures, making Norway an unforgettable destination for travelers seeking natural beauty, history, and cultural experiences.

Day By Day Itinerary

Itinerary description

Day 1: Arrival in Bergen

Arrive in Bergen, the gateway to the Norwegian fjords, and check into your hotel. Spend the day exploring the UNESCO-listed Bryggen Wharf, visit the Bergenhus Fortress, and take a ride on the Fløibanen funicular for panoramic views of the city.

Day 2: Geirangerfjord

Take a scenic drive to Geiranger, a picturesque village located at the head of the UNESCO-listed Geirangerfjord. Enjoy a fjord cruise to experience the breathtaking beauty of the fjords and visit the nearby viewpoints for stunning photo opportunities.

Day 3: Ålesund

Travel to Ålesund, known for its Art Nouveau architecture and scenic location. Explore the charming streets of the city, visit the Jugendstilsenteret museum, and hike up to the Aksla viewpoint for panoramic views of the surrounding islands and fjords.

Day 4: Oslo

Travel to Oslo, the capital city of Norway. Explore the cultural attractions of the city, visit the Viking Ship Museum to see well-preserved Viking ships, stroll through Vigeland Park, and visit the Oslo Opera House for stunning views of the Oslofjord.

Day 5: Departure from Bergen

Enjoy a final breakfast at your hotel before departing for your return journey from Bergen. Transfer to the airport for your flight back home with unforgettable memories of Norway.
