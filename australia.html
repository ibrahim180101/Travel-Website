Day By Day Itinerary

Itinerary description

Day 1: Arrival At The Land Of Beautiful Australia

Your evening is free, hence, you can experience the beautiful and iconic Skypoint Observation Deck. Remember that it remains closed on all Tuesdays

Day 2: Trip to the Amusing Movie World

  • Start with a scrumptious breakfast because an amusing day awaits us!
  • Start for Movie World, which is indeed one of a one-of-a-kind experience. It is the most beautiful movie theme park, specifically if you have kids. There are tons of thrilling rides and captivating shows you can experience.
  • Experience the ultimate fascinating roller coaster rides. Get ready to meet all the Warner Bros characters as well.
  • Stay overnight at the Gold Coast Hotel.

    • Day 3: Experience The Exotic SeaWorld

  • Start the next trip day with a royal breakfast.
  • Visit the Sea World to witness the enigma of the water world.
  • Get ready for the most beautiful theme park journey, as this one is built on the concept of sea animals like dolphins, seals, penguins, and many more!
  • Enjoy many thrilling rides, and you will be in for a day.
  • Retire for the night at the beautiful and comfortable Gold Coast Hotel.

    • Day 4: Prepare To Reach Cairns

  • Start with a heartwarming breakfast
  • You will be dropped off at the Gold Coast Airport for your flight to Cairns Domestic Airport
  • Stay overnight at the Cairns Hotel

    • Day 5: Hop Around The Beautiful Cairns

  • Fuel yourself for extensive Cairns activities with a great breakfast
  • Arrive at the beautiful Kuranda Village, which is extremely picturesque, and you will be treated to a unique view.
  • Arrive at the exclusive Kuranda Village on an exciting Skyrail. The experience is one of a kind. and you will be able to feel what it feels like to be in the sky!
  • Explore around the village. You can also board the Skyrail and then come down.
  • Bid adieu to the thrilling day with an overnight stay

    • Day 6: Enjoy Around The City Of Sydney

  • Fuel yourself with a hearty breakfast.
  • Start the fun exploration by heading to the nearest bus stop for a 24-hour Classic Hop-on-Hop-off Bus Pass. This is the ultimate and perfect way to explore Sydney at your own pace.
  • Visit the world-renowned Sydney Opera House. Undoubtedly, this structure resonates with being a masterpiece in architecture and culture. You will be in for a treat as you witness the captivating 1-hour inside Guided Tour, unveiling the secrets of this magnificent venue.
  • Enjoy a scrumptious lunch at any of the amazing eateries in Sydney. Visit the beautiful Darling Harbour. Exchange your Combo 3 Pass voucher at Sea Life Aquarium.
  • What does this pass bring on? It offers three admissions from a selection of incredible attractions. Your options include Sydney Wildlife, Madame Tussauds, Sea Life Sydney Aquarium, or Sydney Tower Eye with the thrilling 4D Experience.

    • Day 7: Experience The Mystic Blue Mountain

  • Start your day with a great breakfast, and get ready to explore and experience the majestic Blue Mountains.
  • Not only visit the Blue Mountains, there are many more thrilling adventures! It includes visiting Featherdale Wildlife Park, where you can get up close and personal with Australia's unique and adorable wildlife.
  • Get ready as you move and enjoy breathtaking scenic rides at Scenic World. The thrilling cable car and railway experiences here are not to be missed!
  • The adventure continues as you cruise along the mystic Parramatta River.
  • Finally, the trip ends with the King Street Wharf or Circular Quay.
  • Overnight stay at the beautiful Sydney city.

    • Day 8: Arrive At Melbourne

  • After breakfast, transfer to the airport as your flight to Melbourne is scheduled.
  • After check-in, experience the sporting heritage of Melbourne with a guided 1-hour tour of the Melbourne Cricket Ground. Explore the iconic venue that has hosted countless legendary sporting moments.
  • Head to the Melbourne Observation Skydeck for breathtaking panoramic city views. The experience will surely leave you completely mesmerized.
  • Return to the hotel or engage in some fun activities in Melbourne.

  • Start with a good breakfast as a day with the wildlife awaits us.
  • Witness the Penguins at the exotic Philip Islands. You can also visit the Moonlit Sanctuary. You will have the ultimate opportunity to meet and learn about Australia's unique wildlife. Experience the adorable kangaroos, wallabies, and many more fascinating creatures. As the sun sets, watch the scintillating penguin parade for the most spectacular view of a lifetime.
  • Return to the hotel with a heartful of memories and retire for the night.

  • With an amazing breakfast your day starts for sightseeing in Melbourne.
