Day By Day Itinerary

Itinerary description

Day 1: Arrival In Tbilisi

Upon your arrival at Tbilisi International Airport, you will be warmly greeted, and then you can travel to your hotel in the heart of Tbilisi. Enjoy leisure time by going through the city or relaxing. Your overnight stay sets the tone for an exciting journey in Tbilisi.

Day 2: A Tale Of Ananuri, Gudauri, And Kazbegi

Start your adventure with a delicious breakfast at the hotel, setting the tone for an exploration-filled day. Start your 2-hour drive to Kazbegi making some stops along the way. Have a look at the Ananuri Zhinvali Reservoir and pause at the iconic friendship monument in Gudauri. After reaching Kazbegi, the journey continues with a 4x4 vehicle journey to Gergeti. offering views. This day promises a perfect blend of cultural scenes and natural wonders, ensuring memories last a lifetime Return to the comfort of your hotel for a restful overnight stay, reflecting on the day's incredible experiences

Day 3: A Visit To The Old World

  • Start with a delightful breakfast at the hotel. followed by a visit to Old Tbilisi. This 3-4 hour walking tour shows you the city's rich history and includes must-visit landmarks
  • Have a look at the Metekhi Church, which is on a cliff overlooking the Mtkvari River, and soak in the views from the ancient Narikala Fortress.
  • Witness the atmosphere of the 17th-century Sulfur Old Baths adorned with red brick domes
  • Go to Chardin Street (optional) to find cafes, clubs, art salons, and galleries named in honor of the renowned French artist Jean-Baptiste-Simeon Chardin
  • The journey continues to the Peace Bridge. a bow-shaped pedestrian bridge glittering with LEDs, connecting Rike Park with the Old Town
  • End your day with a journey to Mtatsminda Park
  • Return to the hotel for a restful overnight stay.

Day 4: City Of Love

Have breakfast at the hotel, and get ready for a journey to the Kakheti region. a 2-hour drive from Tbilisi. Have a tour of Kakheti, highlighted by a visit to the esteemed KTW Winery. Try participating in culinary activities, creating unforgettable memories Go through the city of love. Sighanghi, with leisure time to uncover its romantic charm Visit the Bodbe. the resting place of St. Nino, before concluding the day Get back to Tbilisi and relax with an overnight stay.

Day 5: The Ancient Capital

Start with breakfast at the hotel and set for a journey to Mtskheta. Georgia's ancient capital. Explore the historical treasures of Mtskheta before continuing the expedition to the mesmerizing Uplistsikhe Cave Town. Look at the secrets of this ancient city through a guided tour End the day with a journey back to Tbilisi where a restful overnight stay awaits

Day 6: Homeward Journey

Begin with a breakfast at the hotel, followed by a searjess check-out process The departure journey starts with a visit to Tbilisi International Airport, ensuring a comfortable and timely transition.
