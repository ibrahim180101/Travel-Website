7-Day Tour of Austria

Austria, known for its stunning Alpine landscapes, rich history, and cultural heritage, is a top destination for travelers seeking natural beauty and vibrant cities. From the imperial grandeur of Vienna to the charming streets of Salzburg, Austria offers a diverse range of experiences. Explore magnificent palaces, such as Schönbrunn Palace and Hofburg Palace, immerse yourself in classical music history with visits to Mozart's birthplace and the Vienna State Opera, and enjoy outdoor activities in the Austrian Alps. Indulge in delicious Austrian cuisine, from Wiener Schnitzel to Sachertorte, and experience the country's renowned hospitality, making Austria an unforgettable destination for your next adventure.

Day By Day Itinerary

Itinerary description

Day 1: Arrival in Vienna

Arrive in Vienna, the capital city of Austria, and check into your hotel. Spend the day exploring the historic center of Vienna, visit St. Stephen's Cathedral, and take a leisurely stroll along the Ringstrasse boulevard.

Day 2: Vienna

Explore more of Vienna's cultural attractions today. Visit Schönbrunn Palace, home to the Habsburg monarchs, tour the Vienna State Opera, and immerse yourself in the art and history of the Kunsthistorisches Museum.

Day 3: Salzburg

Take a train to Salzburg, birthplace of Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart and setting for "The Sound of Music." Explore the charming Old Town, visit Mozart's birthplace, and hike up to Hohensalzburg Fortress for panoramic views of the city.

Day 4: Salzburg

Continue exploring Salzburg today. Take a Sound of Music tour to visit filming locations from the iconic movie, stroll through Mirabell Gardens, and indulge in Mozartkugel chocolates at Café Fürst.

Day 5: Innsbruck

Travel to Innsbruck, a picturesque city nestled in the heart of the Austrian Alps. Explore the historic Old Town, visit the Golden Roof, and take a cable car ride up to the Nordkette mountain range for breathtaking views.

Day 6: Salzkammergut

Embark on a day trip to the Salzkammergut region, famous for its stunning lakes and picturesque villages. Visit Hallstatt, a UNESCO World Heritage site, take a boat ride on Lake Wolfgang, and explore the charming town of St. Wolfgang.

Day 7: Departure from Vienna

Enjoy a final breakfast at your hotel before departing for your return journey from Vienna. Transfer to the airport for your flight back home with unforgettable memories of Austria.
