7-Day Tour of Portugal

Portugal, renowned for its rich history, vibrant culture, and stunning coastlines, stands as a top tourist destination. From the charming streets of Lisbon to the picturesque vineyards of Douro Valley, Portugal offers a captivating journey through its diverse regions. Explore iconic landmarks such as the Belém Tower, São Jorge Castle, and the beautiful beaches of the Algarve. Indulge in delicious Portuguese cuisine, from fresh seafood to pastéis de nata, and immerse yourself in the country's cultural heritage, making Portugal an unforgettable destination for travelers seeking history, natural beauty, and culinary experiences.

Day By Day Itinerary

Itinerary description

Day 1: Arrival in Lisbon

Arrive in Lisbon, the capital city of Portugal, and check into your hotel. Spend the day exploring the historic neighborhoods of Alfama and Baixa, visit iconic landmarks such as the Belém Tower and Jerónimos Monastery.

Day 2: Sintra

Take a day trip to Sintra, a UNESCO World Heritage site known for its romantic palaces and lush gardens. Visit Pena Palace, Quinta da Regaleira, and explore the charming streets of the historic center.

Day 3: Porto

Travel to Porto, Portugal's second-largest city, famous for its port wine production and stunning riverfront. Explore the Ribeira district, visit the Livraria Lello bookstore, and take a boat tour along the Douro River.

Day 4: Douro Valley

Embark on a day trip to the Douro Valley, one of the oldest wine regions in the world. Enjoy wine tastings at local vineyards, take a scenic train ride through the valley, and admire the breathtaking landscapes.

Day 5: Coimbra

Head to Coimbra, home to one of the oldest universities in Europe. Explore the university campus, visit the Joanina Library, and stroll through the charming streets of the historic center.

Day 6: Algarve

Travel to the Algarve region, famous for its stunning beaches and picturesque coastal towns. Relax on the golden sands of Praia da Marinha, explore the caves of Benagil, and enjoy fresh seafood at a local restaurant.

Day 7: Departure from Lisbon

Enjoy a final breakfast at your hotel before departing for your return journey from Lisbon. Transfer to the airport for your flight back home with unforgettable memories of Portugal.
