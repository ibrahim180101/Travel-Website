5-Day Tour of Denmark

Denmark, known for its rich history, innovative design, and picturesque landscapes, is a charming destination waiting to be explored. From the colorful streets of Copenhagen to the serene beauty of the countryside, Denmark offers a delightful journey through its diverse regions. Discover iconic landmarks such as Nyhavn, Kronborg Castle, and Legoland. Indulge in delicious Danish cuisine, from smørrebrød to æbleskiver, and immerse yourself in the country's cultural heritage, making Denmark an unforgettable destination for travelers seeking history, design, and natural beauty.

Day By Day Itinerary

Itinerary description

Day 1: Arrival in Copenhagen

Arrive in Copenhagen, the capital city of Denmark, and check into your hotel. Spend the day exploring the historic streets of Nyhavn, visit the famous Little Mermaid statue, and enjoy a canal boat tour to see the city from a different perspective.

Day 2: Copenhagen

Explore more of Copenhagen's attractions today. Visit Tivoli Gardens, one of the oldest amusement parks in the world, explore the exhibits at the National Museum of Denmark, and indulge in Danish pastries at a local bakery.

Day 3: Odense

Take a train to Odense, the birthplace of Hans Christian Andersen and known for its charming old town. Visit the Hans Christian Andersen Museum, stroll through the cobblestone streets, and explore the picturesque Fyn Village Open-Air Museum.

Day 4: Aarhus

Travel to Aarhus, Denmark's second-largest city, known for its vibrant arts scene and innovative architecture. Visit the ARoS Aarhus Art Museum, explore the old town of Den Gamle By, and relax in the lush surroundings of the Botanical Garden.

Day 5: Departure from Copenhagen

Enjoy a final breakfast at your hotel before departing for your return journey from Copenhagen. Transfer to the airport for your flight back home with unforgettable memories of Denmark.
