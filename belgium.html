Belgium Brussels

Belgium, nestled in the heart of Europe, enchants visitors with its charming blend of medieval architecture, vibrant cities, and delectable chocolates. The UNESCO-listed Grand Place in Brussels stands as a testament to the country's architectural prowess, while the medieval city of Bruges, with its winding canals and cobblestone streets, offers a picturesque journey through time. Belgium's reputation as the world's chocolate capital is further exemplified by its numerous chocolate shops, inviting travellers to indulge in the sweet pleasures of this enchanting destination.

Day 2: Brussels

Brussels After breakfast, enjoy a City Sightseeing tour in the heart of Europe in Brussels. With 13 Hop on Hop off stops conveniently located around the city, you can travel to points of interest and the city's attractions at your leisure. Brussels is home to the European Union with the European Parliament, Manneken Pis, Cinquantenaire Arch, Art Nouveau and Victor Horta, the Grand-Place and the Atomium. The city abounds in architectural monuments, from the Renaissance to modern times and discovering this heritage is full of surprises. Overnight in Brussels.
